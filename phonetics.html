Phonetics

Letters by themselves are consonants.

Letter Sound
< ʃ
> ʒ
^ k
v ɡ

Letters grouped in two are called pairs. First sound of pair is first letter sound, second sound is determined with the following vowel chart:

First letter Second letter/Sound
< > ^ v
< i y ɘ ʊ
> æ ɤ̞ o̞ invalid
^ invalid invalid ɶ ɒ
v invalid invalid invalid ɛ

There shouldn't be any pairs that have invalid vowels.

Due to restrictions here, all of the words will mostly consist of pairs, only the last sound can be a single consonant
